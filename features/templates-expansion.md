# Templates Expansion

## What
Added 50 new SVG drawing templates to bring the total from 34 to 84 templates.

## How
- All templates are monochrome SVG files in `public/templates/` with `viewBox="0 0 800 600"`, `#888` strokes, `stroke-width="2.5"`, and low `fill-opacity` (0.03-0.08)
- No text elements in any template
- Templates registered in the `TEMPLATES` constant in `src/app/components.tsx`
